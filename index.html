01 — About

Who
I am.

I'm an audio professional, entrepreneur, and community builder passionate about creating experiences and products that make a real impact.

As Director of Site Audio at Crossroads Church, I lead FOH engineering across multiple campuses — guiding staff and volunteers, integrating immersive L-ISA systems, and building the culture and standards that make world-class production possible week after week.

Beyond the church, I founded Rapid Adapt LLC — a startup designing patent-pending accessibility products. Think machined aluminum pedal extensions that give shorter drivers comfort, safety, and independence behind the wheel. We're gearing up for crowdfunding and investor partnerships.

Whether it's mixing audio for thousands, prototyping adaptive hardware, or performing on stage — the mission is always the same: empower people and bring them closer to what matters most.
