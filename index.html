01 — About

Who
I am.

I'm an audio professional, entrepreneur, and community builder. I care a lot about creating experiences and products that make a real difference for people.

At Crossroads Church I serve as Director of Site Audio, which means I'm leading FOH engineering across multiple campuses, working alongside staff and volunteers, integrating immersive L-ISA systems, and trying to build a culture where world-class production is just what we do every week.

On the side, I started Rapid Adapt LLC. We're designing patent-pending accessibility products, specifically pedal extensions machined from aluminum that give shorter drivers more comfort, safety, and independence. We're getting ready for crowdfunding and looking for investor partners.

Whether I'm mixing for thousands of people, prototyping hardware in the shop, or performing on stage, the goal is pretty much always the same: help people and bring them closer to what matters.
